Waffle Website

Waffle.com
Waffle.com is a website for people who likes waffle and want to have a snack with friends.
Structure
I chose a neutral white background so the focus points to the waffles. The red touch is eye-catching and perfect to highlight the menu. By clicking to one image the user will see the order page with the photo of the waffle and a list of ingredients to check/uncheck. At the moment of buying them, the customer can specify important details in the text field or just continue and buy.
Research
In the research phase I assessed and compared similar websites where waffles are sold. So I started to distribute images and placed them in a way that can be pleasant for the eye. I gave more importance to the distribution of space and Images instead of text because in a websites where primary goods are sold, images are important for attracting the buyers attention, therefore this factor should be stressed more.

Music Re-composition

Rec-omposition Overview
I decided to create a contrast between dark and light blue forming a line in the middle to juxtapose the two scenes that are part of each other contrasting and integrating at the same time. On the upper part of the design there is a girl that hears music with her headset. Below there is a clearer background made of light blue and dark blue, as if it was painted with the brush , forming almost a puzzle of colors. The intention behind the design is that the music that people hear can heal, accompanying our moods and improving our life. The word "re-composition" symbolizes that the music has the power to making people feel good, relaxed and more focused on the present.

Master Thesis

Description of the thesis
This project was made with the intention of creating a questionnaire about how people perceive the place they live in. The questions were made according to the point of view of students and people that by answering to a first set of questions, gave us the tools on how to build the ambience questionnaire. The three factors that are highlighted in the end of the research are nature attractiveness and unpleasantness. Those 3 factors are the main focus point of the Ambience Questionnaire. The questionnaire can be used to improve urban areas whereas it lacks technologies for its citizens.
Point of view and future suggestions
The questionnaire was made with the intention of evaluating an outside environment but I think that it can be easily applied also for indoors or even for website interface with further research, because everything can give the user perception of ambience.
